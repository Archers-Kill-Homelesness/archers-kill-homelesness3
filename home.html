Archer Houselessness Alliance

The safety of our neighbors is in our hands.

The causes of houselessness vary across many different major cities. However, the major causes transcend geographic boundaries. According to a global study conducted by the Homeless World Cup in 2005, the root causes for homelessness are:

  • Unemployment
  • Lack of Access to Affordable Resources
  • Struggles with Mental Health and Addiction
  • Household Conflicts (commonly found in the LGBTQ+ community)
  • Racial Inequality
  • Domestic Violence
  • Systemic Failures
